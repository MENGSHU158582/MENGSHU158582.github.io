Production & process

After deciding the conecpt,I made a rough sketch in Photoshop to base the composition on.The hunt for photos of hands began,and after collecting quite a few cut them out and placed them into the composition.The fire was created mainly by warping pictures of flamfes onto the shapes I desires.A 3D heart.I used Apophysis to create abstract brought in and manipulated to create the heart .I used motion andtexture in the background,Pixelmator was used to create some final touches,which was brought back ointko Photoshop for final composite.
